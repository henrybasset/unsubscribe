Unsubscribe!  —  How to install  (about 1 minute)


1.  Double-click the file you downloaded (it ends in “.dmg”).
    A small window opens.

2.  Drag the  Unsubscribe!  icon onto the  Applications  folder
    in that same window.

3.  Open your Applications folder and double-click  Unsubscribe!


THE FIRST TIME ONLY — macOS may say it “cannot verify” the app.
That is normal for apps not sold in the App Store. To allow it:

    •  Click  Done  (or  OK)  on the message.
    •  Open the Apple menu  >  System Settings  >  Privacy & Security.
    •  Scroll down until you see a line mentioning  “Unsubscribe!”
    •  Click  Open Anyway,  and confirm with your password or Touch ID.


4.  When it asks for permission to work with Mail, click  OK.

    (If a window offers to install “developer tools,” click  Install.
     It’s a free, one-time download from Apple.)


5.  That’s it!  Look for a small envelope icon in the menu bar at the
    very TOP of your screen, on the right. Click it any time to clean
    up junk mail.
